It's all about inspiration

I'm not joking





The inspiration for this site was originally sourced from an expensive social media marketing template I found at the first stage of this project. The general style - the color scheme, font choice, and overall layout - were all elements that I decided to keep since they fit well with the topic of marketing. The process was certainly long and tedious, but clearly worth the ultimate result.


The Designer

To all who have yet to meet me - my name is Natanel Pinkhasov. While I may seem a mere shadow in the building, I try to avoid letting any of that reflect in my work. To me, putting forward my best effort in nearly everything I do is what makes the difference, especially when it comes to working on something I genuinely enjoy doing. If you’re curious to learn anything more about me, you may feel free to visit my personal landing page or follow me on Instagram.
